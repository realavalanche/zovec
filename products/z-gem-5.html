Z-Gem 5

Z-Gem 5

Zovec’s Z-GEM 5 is an Automated 5-Part Differential Hematology Analyzer based on 3 Angle Laser Scatter Cell measurement, Flow cytometry.

Designed for Fast, Efficient use, Z-GEM 5 provides clinically accurate analysis for patient diagnosis and treatment.
